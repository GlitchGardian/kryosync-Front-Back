About Us

Who We Are

Kryosync is a software development company based in Bhavnagar, Gujarat, founded in August 2024. Led by CEO & Founder Harshil Trivedi and Co-Founder Dharmesh Chauhan, we specialise in web and app development, UI/UX design, SEO, digital marketing, cybersecurity, and more. Our focus is on innovation, quality, and client satisfaction. We transform ideas into impactful digital solutions to help your business succeed.

Partner with us to move your business forward!
